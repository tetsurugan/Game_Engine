# Street RPG — Core Concept (POV add-on lane)
**Package:** Forgotten Trajectory / Street RPG  
**POV role:** Separate **anthology add-on / anchor package** — does not replace Paladin, Gyre, Verge, Rumor Girl, or other core POV lanes.
---
## Premise (3–5 sentences)
**Street RPG** is a narrative-driven urban RPG built around a **dual protagonist** structure: players invest in **MC1 (Rocket Goldenframe)**, then the story reveals the long-arc protagonist is **MC2 (Travis Raisonpointe)**, who has **unknowingly killed his father** while believing he serves justice. The **Antagonist** (blood heir resenting Rocket’s elevation) **grooms** Travis for years using **half-truths** and **mediated lies** (often via **Uncle Smooth** in the merged plot). The **fixed climax** is **patricide** immediately followed by a **desecration / permanent-gold** beat that locks **guilt** and drives the **real** game: **aftermath** — investigation, reputation, family, and **redemption vs collapse**. An **optional higher layer** (**Round Table**, **Construct** / transcended Rocket, **ambiguous message**) remains a **systemic mystery seam**, not required for the public spine.

## Distinction from other POV lanes (identity, not flattening)
| Lane (examples) | Street RPG is not… |
|-----------------|----------------------|
| **Paladin / classical fantasy** | Not that register; **street org rules**, **illegal Goldmouth**, **urban reputation** economics. |

## Fixed spine (must always happen)
1. **MC1** = **Rocket Goldenframe** (legendary leader).  
2. **MC2** = **Travis Raisonpointe** (unknown son, manipulated story).  
3. **Antagonist** **grooms** Travis against Rocket over a long arc.  
4. **Climax:** Rocket **defeats / kills** Antagonist; **trigger** causes **Travis to kill Rocket** (patricide).  
5. **Desecration beat:** **gold** / **permanent** teeth discovery — **guilt** hook.  
6. **Aftermath** is the **primary player-driven space** (investigation, family, community, endings).  
7. **Steady** (SIC) **intervenes** so Travis **survives** to aftermath (stand-down / extraction beat).
Unresolved until canon lock (see `street_rpg_open_canon_seams.md`): Rocket’s **literal** death vs **staged** / **Construct**; **exact** timing of Rocket’s **full** knowledge of Travis.

## Core themes
- **Identity** — earned **Goldenframe** vs blood.  
- **Loyalty** — crew, false mentor, truth, chosen family.  
- **Generational cycles** — merit vs blood; weaponized son.  
- **Silence vs truth** — Rocket’s withholding; investigation as play.  
- **Survival vs morality** — org rules, addiction, sacrifice.  
- **Power and corruption** — Round Table / transcendence (optional layer).  
- **Legacy** — chain, community impact, not inherited legend (standard good outcome text).
