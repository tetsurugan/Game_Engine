## Family A — Redemption / earned legacy
**Meaning:** Travis earns belonging and the **chain** through deeds and family/community gates.
**Belongs here:** “The Chain of Legacy”; secret **additive** content in `ENDING_VARIANTS` that still assumes that resolution; **Ghost Scene** / family embrace **beats** in `MC2_FINAL_ENDINGS` (same value cluster, different scene).
**Values:** Protection, forgiveness, service, chosen family.
**POV:** Strong — continuation, **witness** (Elena, David, community), relationship fallout. Faction aftermath medium. Systemic overlay optional if flags allow.
---
## Family B — Collapse / disposable statistic
**Meaning:** Reject redemption; die as replaceable street casualty; Round Table never known (per bad ending text).
**Belongs here:** “Another Cog in the Wheel.”
**Values:** Denial of growth, power for status, moral collapse to statistic.
**POV:** Weak continuation (PC dead); medium witness / indifferent city; low systemic overlay in source (explicitly hidden).
---
## Family C — Secret / systemic overlay
**Meaning:** Construct / Transcended Rocket, secret boss, memory sequence + erasure, RT coin, ambiguous letter, faked public death in refined secret doc.
**Belongs here:** `SECRET_BOSS_SYSTEM`, `POST_BOSS_MEMORY_SEQUENCE`, `SECRET_ENDING_REFINED`, `AMBIGUOUS_MESSAGE…`, relevant blocks of `MC2_FINAL_ENDINGS`, `MASTER` Construct sketch.
**Values:** Truth vs ignorance, power as cage, personal peace vs cosmic ambiguity.
**POV:** Primary home for **systemic** add-on; continuation conditional on seam locks; **witness** as unreliable; faction/system verbs in master doc are design space, not all shipped.
---
## Family D — Interpretation / epistemic (not always a build branch)
**Meaning:** Competing in-fiction explanations (Construct real vs psych vs RT test).
**Source:** `MC2_FINAL_ENDINGS` layered mystery; `AMBIGUOUS_MESSAGE` list.
**POV:** Community/theory space; not MVP branch explosion — one **policy** per build target.
---
**Summary:** Plan **A + B** first; **C + D** as optional add-on tracks until seams lock.
