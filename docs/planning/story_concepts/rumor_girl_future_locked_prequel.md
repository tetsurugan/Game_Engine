# Story concept — Rumor Girl **future-locked prequel** (companion romance chapter)

**Status:** Planning / archive — **not** implemented. **Depends on shipped** [`rumor_girl`](../../../src/stories/rumor_girl/story.ts) for **retroactive closure** design.  
**Era:** Modern (same lane as `rumor_girl`; [`eras_and_scales.md`](../eras_and_scales.md) — `modern_fractured_court_media_ai`).  
**Engine fit:** [`design_doctrine.md`](../design_doctrine.md), **[`branch_scope_doctrine.md`](../branch_scope_doctrine.md)** — emotional richness via **variants inside a few closure buckets**, not one sequel per `rumor_girl` ending.

---

## What “future-locked prequel” means here

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Chronology** | This story happens **earlier** in the relationship graph: high school / college / first jobs — before the breakup table of `rumor_girl`. |
| **Play order** | The player **unlocks or completes** it **after** having played `rumor_girl` (or at least after the anthology **knows** a `rumor_girl` **outcome family**). |
| **Closure model** | The prequel’s **final emotional closure is not fully decided inside the prequel alone**. **`rumor_girl` chooses the closure *bucket*** (what kind of life/death/aftermath the triangle eventually became). **Prequel choices + personality choose the *flavor*** inside that bucket. |
| **Retroactive closure** | Finishing `rumor_girl` first **backfills** what “helping him get the girl” **meant** in the end — gravesite, court bench, rumor chain, or messy survival. |
| **Companion prequel** | It is **another chapter** of the same moral universe as `rumor_girl`, but **its own** title, POV, and scene list — not a “Part 0” menu label unless UX wants it. |

**Player experience:** On first play of the prequel, the connection to `rumor_girl` can stay **subtle** (names optional, faces generic). After `rumor_girl`, **reveal** lands as **deepening** tragedy or irony — **not** a retcon of `rumor_girl`’s hidden/belief logic.

## Core concept (grounded, human-scale)

**POV:** A young woman **secretly in love** with her **shy male best friend**. He is fixated on **another girl** — who, in anthology continuity, may later be the **`rumor_girl` protagonist** (the girlfriend at the breakup table). The POV girl **helps him anyway**: drafts texts, covers for him, rehearses him, lends him confidence.

**Tone:** Modern, intimate, awkward, **not** supernatural by default. The pain is **loyalty misapplied**, **hope deferred**, **competence used as self-harm**.

**She may, across the prequel:**
- Stay **loyal** to the bit (best friend first, crush buried).
- **Almost confess** — pull back, laugh it off, hate herself.
- **Quietly destabilize** — small sabotage dressed as “honest advice.”
- **Cross a line once** — screenshot, rumor, “accidental” leak, boundary break.
- Become **“good to both of them”** — mediator poison, everyone’s therapist, no one’s beloved.
- **Distance herself too late** — already woven into their origin story.

**Shy guy (morally interesting, not cartoon evil):** He can be **sincerely fond** of the POV girl in one register (safety, history, gratitude) while **romantically chasing** the other girl. He may be **trying to be good** — honest enough not to lead the POV girl on, **weak enough** to accept endless help anyway. He may be **physically loyal** early while **emotionally drifting**. He may **mean well** and still **manufacture wreckage** by waiting too long to name what he feels. This keeps **`rumor_girl`**’s later mess **human** — not a monster upgrade.

## Hidden connection to `rumor_girl` (planning canon, soft on first contact)

**Default anthology mapping (authoring may vary):**

| Prequel figure | Later role in `rumor_girl` |
|----------------|----------------------------|
| Shy best friend | The **boyfriend** at the apartment — same person, younger. |
| Target girl (crush) | The **`rumor_girl` protagonist** — same person, earlier arc. |
| POV helper | Later: **friend**, **rumor source**, **witness**, **mourner**, **court witness**, **messy third point**, or **rare “still in his life”** option — **flavor** decided by prequel + `rumor_girl` **bucket**. |

**Rules:**
- The link can be **easter-egg subtle** in copy (no mandatory name drop).
- **`rumor_girl` remains authoritative** for the breakup night’s **hidden truth** (cheat, accountable break, non-mockery) — the prequel does **not** rewrite that module.
- Prequel adds **emotional archaeology** only.

## Closure families (small — variants inside, not sequel trees)

Per **[`branch_scope_doctrine.md`](../branch_scope_doctrine.md):** *not every emotional ending deserves a sequel*; *variants preferred over infinite branching*. Here, **four** closure **families** total. **`rumor_girl` picks the family** after play; **prequel state picks the variant**.

### 1. `rumor_prequel_closure_love_bad` — **Love won, but badly**

**Emotional meaning:** The triangle **resolved into romance** between him and the crush — or a **parody** of it — with the POV girl **inside the wreckage** (enabler, resentful bridesmaid, “sister,” or secret third rail). **Bitter loyalty**, **unfinished confession**, **intimacy without legitimacy**.

**`rumor_girl` buckets that feed this (examples):**
- Grounded endings where the couple **stays** or **dies slowly** together: `stay_anyway`, `denial_haze`, `mixed_death`, `suspicion_poison` — **shared history** still toxic or glued.
- **Not** the cleanest `leave_confirmed` unless flavor is “she helped build the couple that then rotted.”

**Variants (flavors inside family):**
- She **never stopped** helping; watches the affair era from inside the friend perimeter.
- She **confessed once**; he **gentle-rejected**; she **stayed** as friend — later `rumor_girl` proves that wasn’t stable.
- **Good couple / shitty couple** sub-flavors — same **family**, different epilogue lines and echo tags.

---

### 2. `rumor_prequel_closure_witness_web` — **Witness / aftermath role**

**Emotional meaning:** The POV girl becomes part of the **public or legal web** around the later story — **credible witness**, **rumor origin**, **character witness**, **person who “knew them when.”**

**`rumor_girl` buckets that feed this:**
- **`rumor_girl_court_seed_stack`** echo and/or **`rumor_girl_witness_aftermath_invited`** flag — **court / statement** lane.
- **`rumor_girl_belief_gap_soft`** — record-vs-POV contrast; she may testify to **early pattern** of his conflict avoidance.
- Grounded **`leave_confirmed`**, **`self_first`** — she’s **alive and talkative** in the social graph.

**Variants:**
- Clerk-of-the-heart: **reluctant** witness vs **vengeful** clarity.
- **Rumor source** who didn’t mean to **weaponize** — variant intros for dialogue only.

---

### 3. `rumor_prequel_closure_unpaid_thread` — **Never truly became a couple (unpaid thread)**

**Emotional meaning:** Romance between POV girl and him **never realized**; she **exits** or **dims** with a **scar**, not a body count. **Platonic grief**, **timing**, **pride**.

**`rumor_girl` buckets that feed this:**
- **`inward_collapse`**, **`self_first`** on his partner’s side — mirrors **distance** theme.
- **`leave_confirmed`** — clean break culture; prequel girl may have **walked early** or **never been chosen**.

**Variants:**
- She **distanced too late** — still dragged into `rumor_girl` **as friend**.
- **Proud suppressor** arc: clean surface, **unpaid** interior.

---

### 4. `rumor_prequel_closure_horror_residue` — **Horror spillover / gravesite / court residue**

**Emotional meaning:** **`rumor_girl` shock path** spills backward: the **helpful best friend** is now **mourner**, **suspect-adjacent**, **person who knew his voice**, or **gravesite regular**. **Tragedy retroactively stains** the prequel’s sweet scenes.

**`rumor_girl` buckets that feed this:**
- Mark **`rumor_girl_fatal_aftermath_seeded`** + echo **`rumor_girl_shock_her_pov`** — **mandatory** alignment for this family’s **primary** variant set.

**Variants (still one family):**
- **Gravesite** flavor vs **court gallery** flavor vs **police waiting room** flavor — **template / scene swaps**, not three new module rows.
- **She did not cause it** vs **she fears she enabled** — belief-layer flavor in a later witness beat.

## Mapping: `rumor_girl` → prequel closure **bucket** (not 1:1 per ending)

**Rule:** Map **`rumor_girl` outcome groups** to **exactly one** prequel closure **family**. Prequel runtime (personality, flags, echoes from prequel play) selects **variant**.

| `rumor_girl` outcome group (bucket) | Member signals (implementation-facing) | Prequel closure **family** |
|-------------------------------------|----------------------------------------|----------------------------|
| **A — Grounded, couple stress / glue** | Endings `stay_anyway`, `denial_haze`, `mixed_death`, `suspicion_poison`; echoes accordingly; **no** fatal mark | `rumor_prequel_closure_love_bad` |
| **B — Grounded, exit / distance** | `leave_confirmed`, `self_first`; optional `rumor_girl_belief_gap_soft` | `rumor_prequel_closure_witness_web` **or** `rumor_prequel_closure_unpaid_thread` (choose by **prequel** flavor: witness-capable vs self-erasing — **one** secondary rule in implementation) |
| **C — Grounded, inward / unpaid** | `inward_collapse` | `rumor_prequel_closure_unpaid_thread` |
| **D — Shock / fatal seed** | Mark **`rumor_girl_fatal_aftermath_seeded`** + shock echoes | `rumor_prequel_closure_horror_residue` |

**Note:** Bucket **B** may need a **single** disambiguation rule (e.g. prequel personality `proud_suppressor` → `unpaid_thread`; `playful_denier` with `prequel_crossed_line_once` mark → `witness_web`) — **data-driven**, not a new sequel.

**Forbidden:** 9 prequel modules for 9 `rumor_girl` endings.

## Prequel personality stances (flavor drivers)

| Stance id (proposed) | Name | Prequel behavior | Reads shy guy as… | Destabilize triangle? | Closure **lean** (not lock) |
|----------------------|------|------------------|-------------------|------------------------|-----------------------------|
| `prequel_sacrifice` | Self-sacrificing romantic | Helps **openly**, pain buried | “He deserves the win” | Low unless bottled | `love_bad`, `unpaid_thread` |
| `prequel_jealous_loyal` | Jealous loyalist | Helps **with teeth**; tests boundaries | “If I’m good enough he’ll see” | Medium | `love_bad`, `witness_web` |
| `prequel_playful_deny` | Playful denier | Jokes away crush; “we’re ridiculous” | Safe, asexual box | Low–medium | `unpaid_thread`, `witness_web` |
| `prequel_quiet_desperate` | Quietly desperate | Over-help, over-available | Only she **really** knows him | Medium–high | `love_bad`, **horror residue** if RG shock |
| `prequel_proud_suppressor` | Proud suppressor | Competent, cold surface; burns inside | Shame if she asks | Medium (passive-aggressive) | `unpaid_thread`, `witness_web` |

**Implementation note:** Personalities skew **variant** probabilities; **bucket** still from `rumor_girl`.

## Gravesite / court / romance as **flavors**

- **Gravesite mourner**, **court witness**, **they end up together**, **good couple**, **shitty couple** = **variant intros + echo stacks** inside **`horror_residue`** or **`love_bad`** or **`witness_web`** — **not** five top-level sequel modules.
- [`branch_scope_doctrine.md`](../branch_scope_doctrine.md): prefer **`template_variant`** + **witness_fragment** over **`real_new_module`** for each flavor.

## Doctrine alignment

- **[`branch_scope_doctrine.md`](../branch_scope_doctrine.md):** Default **memory-first**; **one** future-locked prequel row, **four** closure families, **variants** inside — **not** a combinatorial sequel list.
- **Emotional richness ≠ branch explosion:** `rumor_girl` already has many endings; prequel **reuses** grouped buckets.
- **Related shipped module:** [`rumor_girl`](../../../src/stories/rumor_girl/story.ts); **registry** concept row: `rumor_girl_future_locked_prequel` (when added).
